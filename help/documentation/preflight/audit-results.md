# Audit results in Preflight

When the audit completes, Preflight displays the audit results as opportunities. Each opportunity is organized by type and includes recommendations to help you enhance and optimize the page.

At the top of the AEM Preflight dialog is a User Progress bar that reflects overall audit results. It shows the percentage of opportunities that passed with no issues, and the total number of issues found across all opportunities. The User Progress bar helps authors gauge overall page health at a glance. The bar is color-coded: 
    
* Red for **less than 1/3** of opportunities complete
* Orange for **1/3 to 2/3 complete**
* Green for **more than 2/3 complete**
* Blue while audits are **still running**

See the [complete list of available opportunity types and how to address them](./overview.md#preflight-opportunities).
