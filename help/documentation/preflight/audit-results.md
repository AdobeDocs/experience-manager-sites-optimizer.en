# Audit results in Preflight

When the audit completes, Preflight displays the audit results as opportunities. Each opportunity is organized by type and includes recommendations to help you enhance and optimize the page. Within an opportunity, individual issues identify specific items to review or fix.

At the top of the AEM Preflight dialog is a User Progress bar that reflects overall audit results. It shows the percentage of opportunities that passed with no issues, and the total number of issues found across all opportunities. The User Progress bar helps authors gauge overall page health at a glance.

![User Progress bar and audit opportunities in the AEM Preflight dialog](./assets/overview/hero.png){align="center"}

The bar is color-coded:

* Red for **less than 1/3** of opportunities complete
* Orange for **1/3 to 2/3 complete**
* Green for **more than 2/3 complete**
* Blue while audits are **still running**

See the [complete list of available opportunity types and how to address them](./overview.md#preflight-opportunities).

## Navigate to issues

After the audit completes, you can move quickly to identified issues in the preview.

![Preflight preview highlighting an issue](./assets/audit-results/highlight-issue.png){align="center"}

### Navigate to an issue

1. Select an issue from the issues list in the Preflight panel.
1. The preview automatically scrolls to and highlights the corresponding location on the page, so you can review the issue in context without manually searching for it.
