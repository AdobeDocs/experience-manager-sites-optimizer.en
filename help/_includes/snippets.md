## Request approval {#auto-optimize-request-approval}

If you are not able to deploy the optimization, you can request approval from your AEM Site's administrator. Selecting **Request approval** sends an email to the AEM Site's administrator with the details of the optimization. The administrator can then approve or reject the optimization.

## Deploy optimization {#auto-optimize-deploy-optimization-slack}

Adobe's team is here to help! For support with technical optimizations, join Adobe's Slack channel and connect directly with Adobe experts.
